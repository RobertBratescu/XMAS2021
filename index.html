Believing in Santa

Our 2021 Christmas project

Even if the weather outside is not frightful and the fire inside is not yet delightful, that joyous time of the year is approaching. A time that is rather made wonderful by the ones that allow kindness and positivity to turn everything into magic. The true spirit of Christmas lies, after all, in each and every one of us, which is why we should also aspire to fill the hearts of the less fortunate ones with joy and hope for the future.

As we remembered your last year’s excitement for the “Santa Clause for the Community” project, we thought we should put on our Santa suit once again to bring a smile on a handful of young faces.

As you know, our project is not conducted by adults, but by children aged 14 to 18, who took the matters of social justice, at times of Christmas, in their own hands. And, with the help of A.p.E.A.L (Asociatia pentru Educatie, Antreprenoriat si Leadership) they made an educative experience out of it. Santa Clause for the Community means that Santa's little helpers are youngsters from Ciocănești, Dambovița County, who:

Develop social inquiries by going door to door, in order to evaluate which children are eligible for the project
Organize the collection of letters for Santa
Keep close contact with the do-gooders
Organize the celebration for Santa, to welcome him in rural communities from Ciocanesti, Urziceanca and Vizuresti, Dambovita County
Finalize the project by recognizing and individually confirming each participating Santa.
